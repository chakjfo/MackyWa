CAREER DESCRIPTION

I've been in this industry for about four years, but I started doing training for almost nine years. At first, I was really into performing, mainly dancing, but there are other skills I have discovered, such as my modeling edge. I have experience walking on runways as an ambassador of my previous modeling agency and was acknowledged for participating in more fashion shows. My passion for the runway made me feel incomplete. That's why I auditioned for go see advertisement. At first, it was slow progress, but in the long run, I am blessed to have tons of advertisement projects, and I can show off.
